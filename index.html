Verification Framework

Your data verified by
the world's most trusted bodies.

Verra
Verified Carbon Standard · Washington D.C. · Est. 2005

The world's leading voluntary carbon crediting programme. Over 1,700 certified projects across 80 countries. Verra-verified credits are the global benchmark for corporate carbon offsetting and net-zero claims.

Why this matters for you Your buyers — in Europe, the US, and the Middle East — recognise Verra VCS credits. When your emissions reductions are Verra-verified, they are internationally tradeable and carry the highest credibility.
Gold Standard
Impact Certified · Geneva · WWF Co-Founded · Est. 2003

Co-founded by WWF and development organisations, Gold Standard is the highest-integrity voluntary carbon and climate impact standard. It verifies not only carbon reductions but sustainable development co-benefits.

Why this matters for you Gold Standard projects command a significant price premium in the voluntary carbon market and are recognised under UNFCCC Article 6. For companies with ambitious ESG commitments, this is the gold benchmark.
BEE India
Bureau of Energy Efficiency · Ministry of Power · New Delhi

India's national energy efficiency authority. Administers the PAT (Perform, Achieve & Trade) scheme, sets industrial energy efficiency targets, manages ESCerts issuance, and empanels third-party verification agencies.

Why this matters for you BEE empanelment is mandatory for any PAT scheme participation. Our framework is designed to produce BEE-acceptable MRV data, ensuring your ESCert eligibility is documented correctly from day one.
